Background on Steve

  • Steve was born in 1955 in San Franciso, California.
  • Steve had a daughter, Lisa, with his girlfriend Chrisann, but denied being the father until Lisa was 7, and Lisa lived with him when she was a teen.
  • Steve Jobs co-founded Apple Computers with Steve Wozniak. He started Apple with Wozniak in 1976.
  • Apple is one of the World's largest technology companies. Steve Jobs helped create the famous iPods, iPhones, and iPads.
  • Steve left Apple in 1985 to begin a new hardware and software enterprise called NeXT, Inc. Apple eventually bought the company in 1996 for $429 million. The following year, Jobs returned to his post as Apple's CEO.
  • In the early 1990s, Jobs met Laurene Powell. They got married on March 18, 1991, and lived in Palo Alto, California with their three children.
  • Steve had pancreatic cancer in 2003, but kept postponing his surgery. Finally in 2004, Steve had a successful surgery to remove the pancreatic tumor.
  • In 2008, iTunes became the second biggest music retailer in America.
  • On October 5, 2011, Steve Jobs passed away.
