Background on Steve

Steve Jobs co-founded Apple Computers with Steve Wozniak. Steve was born in 1955 in San Franciso, California. He started Apple with Wozniak in 1976. Apple is one of the World's largest technology companies. Steve Jobs helped create the famous iPods, iPhones, and iPads.
